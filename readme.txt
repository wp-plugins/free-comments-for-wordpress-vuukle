== Description ==
Vuukle is a social engagement platform especially deisgned for large scale publishers to get the most from your favourite social networks like Facebook, Linkedin, Twitter and Google+.

Our clients include large scale news publishers like http://thehindubusinessline.com, http://financialexpress.com, http://loksatta.com, http://tamil.thehindu.com and many others.

If you are looking for a no hassle social engagement tool for your blog, try out Vuukle today! 

We have recently launched a new feature to personalize content on your site. Currently, this is in private beta for further questions you can reach out to us at support@vuukle.com.

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress and your Done!

OR

If you want to do it through FTP:
1. Upload `vuukle.php` and 'comments.php' to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress and your Done!
